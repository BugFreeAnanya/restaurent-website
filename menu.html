OSHEEN CAFE MENU :

Osheen Restaurant in Hoshangabad offers a diverse menu that includes Chinese, Punjabi, South Indian, North Indian, and Continental dishes, with a wide selection of vegetarian options. The menu features various appetisers like Paneer 65 and Mushroom Chilli, and main courses such as Paneer Lababdar and Dal Makhani. Additionally, they serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner options, including different types of rotis, naans, South Indian dosas, and biryani/rice preparations.
